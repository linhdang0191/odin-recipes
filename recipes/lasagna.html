Lasagna

Description

The most popular lasagna recipe on the internet for two decades.

Making lasagna can be time consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. This recipe is highly customizable.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving

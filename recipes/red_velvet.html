Red Velvet Cake

Description

The best Red Velvet cake ever! Ultra moist, buttery, tender, and soft. Perfect for Valentine!

Ingredients

  • Vegetable oil
  • Plain flour
  • Cocoa powder
  • Baking powder
  • Brown sugar
  • Butter milk
  • Vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Heat the oven
  2. Mix the dry ingredients
  3. Mix the wet ingredients
  4. Mix wet part and dry part together
  5. Bake the sponge cake
  6. Prepare the frosting
  7. Assemble the cake
